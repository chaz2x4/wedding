How We Met

We met at Mr Joseph’s Piano lessons when we were in high school. Haylee came with her friend on a whim - something she had not previously done, because her grandmother would usually pick her up from school.
Chaz had been taking piano lessons with his younger brothers for a few months now. They started talking and immediately hit it off as friends. Haylee would show up every now and again, and after talking for a while about music and other things we had in common, decided to exchange numbers.
Over the years, we would call each other and talk for hours about nothing. Usually via skype, or hangouts or whatever was hip that year. Haylee would also call me from her aunt’s house in Philadelphia; Chaz would hide away in his basement until sun up talking with her. We slowly became best friends.
In 2017, we started talking more and flirting more. We went to the Navy ball and had fun exploring the southern borders. We would always joke around with the idea of ultimately ending up with each other because of how well we click. We never get bored of each other. Who knew that our relationship would move so well, so quickly.
